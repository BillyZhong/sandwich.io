About Sandwich


Sandwich aims to use Speech Recognition to transcribe lectures and then apply Topic Modeling to identify the key topics of the lecture. Then, using our topics, we use tailor google searches to find useful academic resources and use Natural Language Processing to provide quick summaries of the links for easy reading. Finally, if provided with a textbook and or pertinent PPT files, the app will return the related pages in the pdf and ppt's for the user to check out.

How do I use Sandwich?


Well, normally you wouldn't use a sandwich. But feel free to head over to the create tab and record a lecture or upload a transcript. Then, Sandwich will process the lecture and return back all useful information and it will even make it taste good (not really). Enjoy!
